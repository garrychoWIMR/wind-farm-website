About the Lab Study

In the lab component of this study, we will be investigating the short-term effects of infrasound, traffic noise and quiet (a Sham?) on health.

Participants will be asked to attend 3 weekend visits in our sleep centre for a consecutive 72 hours whilst being under constant exposure.

All participants will be monitored throughout each visit and measured for changes in sleep, cognition, cardiovascular health and risk, hearing and balance.
